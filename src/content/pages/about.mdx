### Who Is Dr. Lisa Gold

For nearly three decades, I&rsquo;ve sat with couples and individuals navigating the hardest
moments of their lives — grief, trauma, infidelity, identity, and the quiet existential
questions that arrive in midlife. My work blends the rigor of evidence-based psychotherapy
with the depth of somatic, transpersonal, and psychedelic medicine.
