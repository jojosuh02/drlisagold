## How coaching differs from therapy

Coaching is forward-looking, goal-oriented, and available in all 50 states — unlike licensed
psychotherapy, which is limited to the states in which Dr. Gold holds a license. Coaching
focuses on skill-building, mindset, relationship dynamics, and life transitions rather than
treating diagnosed mental health conditions.

<Callout tone="note" title="Is coaching right for me?">
  Coaching is a fit if you&rsquo;re generally well-resourced and seeking support through a
  transition or growth edge. If you&rsquo;re navigating acute trauma or clinical symptoms,
  psychotherapy (where available) or referral to a local therapist is the better path.
</Callout>
