## Treatment phases

### Phase 1 — Assessment &amp; Stabilization

We begin with an in-depth intake to understand your history, goals, and window of tolerance.
We establish safety, resources, and a shared map of the work ahead.

### Phase 2 — Core Therapy

Depending on your needs, this can include EFT for couples, IFS, Somatic Experiencing, EMDR,
or Ketamine-Assisted Psychotherapy. We move between insight and embodied processing.

### Phase 3 — Integration &amp; Growth

Whether from therapy, a retreat, or an independent psychedelic experience, integration is
where lasting change lives. We weave insights back into your day-to-day life and
relationships.
