## Retreat types

### Soul Surfing: KAP &amp; Psychedelic Healing Experiences

Intimate small-group retreats designed around 2–4 ketamine-assisted sessions, with ample
time for integration, somatic practice, and nature immersion.

### Integrated Intimacy Therapy Retreats

For individuals or couples seeking a concentrated window of relational repair — a week of
daily sessions with Dr. Gold, interspersed with experiential work.

### Couples Intimacy Retreats

A contained space for couples to reconnect, heal ruptures, and rebuild erotic and emotional
intimacy. Typically 5 days, small cohort.
