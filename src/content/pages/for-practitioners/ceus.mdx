## Available hours

Our Soul Surfing foundational training offers 40+ CEU hours. Live consultation groups accrue
CEU hours at a rate of 2 hours per monthly session. Recorded courses are eligible for
self-study credit where permitted.

<Callout tone="note">
  CEU approval varies by state and discipline. Please confirm with your licensing board
  before registering. A certificate of completion is provided for all programs.
</Callout>
